Dal Lake

Location:- Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir, India
Dal is a lake in Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, India. It is the most visited place in srinagar by tourists and locals.View of Dal Lake are completely different in 4 seaons. It is integral to tourism and recreation in Kashmir and is named the "Lake of Flowers", "Jewel in the crown of Kashmir. The lake is also an important source for commercial operations in fishing and water plant harvesting.
